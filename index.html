Bio

Hi,  I am Sadia Masood, I am an energetic creative individual with 9 years’ experience in the media industry under my belt. I continue to dedicate my time in producing professional, innovative and creative solutions for a multitude of clients. I have vast experience in branding, marketing, and integrated communication across print, digital and social platforms. I have always enjoyed interacting with and helping people. I did my Bachelor’s Degree in Mass Communication from Pakistan then I started working with a Government Organization as a Media Consultant.  I believe that perusing the master degree in Design and Media Management from AI Miami International University of Art & Design will add value to my professional skills. Communication can be physical or virtual and may include images, words or graphic forms, to understand more about this world of fantasy I did internships in Advertising and Marketing agencies located in Miami. I feel proud to be a part of Miami Art Fair, I worked as a fair assistant with PULSE Miami 2016. Throughout my education and professional development, I have always been attracted to the interface of Digital Media. The interdisciplinary nature of my work has involved me in many areas of communication, from advertising to Public relations and marketing.  My diverse background has allowed me to apply techniques from many different sub-disciplines of media. This is the recompilation of all the assignments and projects that I have done throughout my education & professional career. I hope that you enjoy knowing more about my professional side !   Thank you….
